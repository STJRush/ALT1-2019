1.1 Research

Our ideas for this project was to make a interactive website that sends data to a firebase

Our website is "Rate my Dream Car". It has a photo of a 1970 Landrover Defender and a rating bar, which the numbers submitted is transfered to a firebase.This is so we know the highest ratings and the lowest ones.
